How it works

Welcome to the Uber Backseat Arcade.

‍It's simple: You have a 10-minute window to rack up points, replay, or switch games. If you score high enough, you'll unlock a promo code to use on your next trip. Claim it by tapping through to your Uber account or forfeit the promo code and play again. Only your latest score applies – so choose wisely. Make sure to claim any promo code you unlock before the countdown times out!

Keep an eye out for the Uber Backseat Arcade banner during Uber trips until 21 December. If you're lucky, you'll get another chance to play!

‍Can't do that if you're driving.
